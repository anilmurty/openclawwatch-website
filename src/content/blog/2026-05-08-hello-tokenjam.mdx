We're starting the TokenJam blog. Expect notes on agent observability, evaluation, environments, gateways, memory, guardrails, HITL, control planes, and cost optimization — written for people who actually build agents in production.
</TLDR>

## Why a blog

Building agentic systems is still mostly tribal knowledge. The patterns that matter — what to instrument, what to evaluate, where to put guardrails, how to budget tokens — aren't yet written down in a way that builders can pick up and apply.

This blog is where we'll write what we learn as we build [TokenJam](https://github.com/metabuilder-labs/tokenjam) and study the surrounding ecosystem. Expect concrete posts, not vendor-speak.

## What you can expect

- **Foundational pieces** on the building blocks of agentic systems.
- **Comparisons** of tools, frameworks, and approaches.
- **Field notes** from real projects.

That's it. More soon.
